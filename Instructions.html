Christmas Lights Bingo Card
 
Each tick box means you need that many to claim that space("elves" ◻ ◻ ◻ needs 3 separate yards with elves to cross off)
If a box has something that can't be found in your area, ex: "Alligators with Santa Hats", agree as a group what to replace it with
Red items are anticipated as being harder, green less hard, and black easiest.
If you have more items you want to see on these cards, contact @jcolebrand
You can play this on your phone at http://j.mp/xmas-light-bingo (warning: refreshing the page will give you a new board!)
